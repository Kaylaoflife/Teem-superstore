Teem Superstore Performance Report

In this project, I explored sales data from Teem Superstore, diving deep into performance trends across regions, product categories, and time periods. I built an interactive Power BI report that gives stakeholders a clear picture of where sales are booming, where they’re falling short, and what can be done about it. This project showcases my ability to turn raw numbers into business-driven insights. From identifying West and East as the highest revenue-generating regions to spotting seasonal sales peaks and underperforming furniture segments, every chart and metric was chosen with a clear goal in mind: helping decision-makers act smartly. It’s a real-world example of how I use data to guide strategy, spot opportunities, and recommend solutions that drive growth.
